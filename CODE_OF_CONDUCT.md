# Code of Conduct

This project expects contributors to:
- Communicate directly and professionally, and assume good faith in others.
- Give and receive technical criticism about the work, not the person.
- Keep discussion on-topic and in the appropriate channel (issue, PR, or team chat).
- Respect that maintainers may close, defer, or redirect contributions that don't fit
  current priorities — disagreement is fine, hostility isn't.

Reports of conduct that falls short of this can be raised with the repo's listed
owner/maintainer or your normal chain of reporting.
